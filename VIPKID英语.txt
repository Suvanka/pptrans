2. How do we use cookies and similar technologies
We or our third-party partners may collect and use your information through cookies, and store such information as log information.
We use our own cookies to provide you with a more personalized user experience and products and/or services, and for the following purposes:
(1) Remember your identity. Cookies help us identify you as our registered user or store information you provide us about your preferences or other information;
(2) Analyze your usage of our products and/or services. We can use cookies to understand what activities you engage in using our products and/or services, or which web pages or products and/or services are most popular with you;
(3) Advertising optimization. Cookies and web beacons help us provide you with advertisements related to our products and/or services based on your information.
While using cookies for the above purposes, we may also use non personally identifiable information collected through cookies to analyze how users use our products and/or services, and for our advertising services. You can refuse or manage cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that if cookies or web beacons are disabled, you may not be able to enjoy the best service experience, and some products and/or services may not function properly. In order to confirm your usage pattern and website usage status, our website will record your device's IP address and browser type, user browsing behavior and operation behavior, etc., to assist us in improving the design and formatting of VIPKID.

c) Delete your personal information
In the following situations, you can request us to delete your personal information:

If we collect and use your personal information without your consent;
If our handling of personal information violates our agreement with you;
If you no longer use our products or services, or if you cancel your VIPKID User Center account;

If we decide to respond to your deletion request, we will also notify the entities that have obtained your personal information from us to delete it in a timely manner, unless otherwise provided by laws and regulations, or if these entities have obtained your independent authorization.
When you delete information from our service, we may not immediately delete the corresponding information from the backup system, but will delete this information when the backup is updated.

Each basic business function requires some basic personal information to be completed. For the collection and use of personal information collected for the purpose of expanding business functions, you can give or revoke your authorization and consent at any time. You can manage your authorization through device system permission settings.
After you withdraw your consent, we will no longer process the corresponding personal information. But your decision to withdraw consent will not affect the personal information processing previously carried out based on your authorization.

After verification, we will handle the corresponding cancellation procedures for you. After the cancellation of your VIPKID User Center account, we will cease to provide you with any VIPKID products and services, and delete your personal information as per your request, except as otherwise provided by laws and regulations.
f) Responding to your request
To ensure safety, you may need to provide a written request or prove your identity in other ways. We may first require you to verify your identity before processing your request. We will provide a response within fifteen days. If you are not satisfied, you can file a complaint through the customer service hotline on the homepage of VIPKID website (www.vipkid. com. cn) or online consultation. For your reasonable requests, we generally do not charge any fees, but for requests that are repeated multiple times or exceed reasonable limits, we will charge a certain cost fee depending on the situation. We may refuse requests that are unnecessarily repetitive, require excessive technical means (such as developing new systems or fundamentally changing current practices), pose risks to the legitimate rights and interests of others, or are highly impractical (such as involving information stored on backup tapes).

We will not be able to respond to your request in the following situations:
a) Related to the fulfillment of legal and regulatory obligations by personal information controllers;
b) Directly related to national security and defense security;
c) Directly related to public safety, public health, and major public interests;
d) Directly related to criminal investigation, prosecution, trial, and execution of judgments;
e) The personal information controller has sufficient evidence to indicate that the personal information subject has subjective malice or abuse of rights;
f) In order to safeguard the significant legitimate rights and interests of personal information subjects or other individuals, such as life and property, but it is difficult to obtain their consent;
g) Responding to the request of the personal information subject will result in serious damage to the legitimate rights and interests of the personal information subject or other individuals or organizations;
h) Involving trade secrets.

7. Protection of Children's Personal Information
We understand that the personal information we collect and process from students may come from minors under the age of 14 ("children"), and we attach great importance to the protection of children's personal information. In this regard, we strictly comply with relevant domestic regulations on personal information protection such as the Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China and the Regulations on the Protection of Children's Personal Information Networks, and fulfill our protection responsibilities. We make the following commitments:
(1) Without the consent of the guardian, children are not allowed to create their own VIPKID user center account;
(2) Before collecting personal information from children, we will remind them and their guardians to read this Privacy Policy, inform them of the scenarios in which we collect and process children's personal information, and the information security protection measures we have taken;
(3) We will only collect and process personal information of children with the authorization and consent of their guardians, and strictly process this part of personal information in accordance with legal provisions with the consent of the guardians or as necessary to protect the children;
(4) We have provided a way for children and their guardians to refuse the above-mentioned collection and processing behaviors. Children and their guardians can contact us through the contact information attached in Article 11 of this Privacy Policy to express specific refusal intentions. We will handle it based on identity recognition;
(5) In general, we do not collect personal information unrelated to the functional scenarios described in this privacy policy; If it is necessary to collect or process beyond the agreed purpose and scope due to business needs, we will specifically inform the child's guardian and obtain their consent;
(6) We will strictly set information access permissions for different functions and control the scope of children's personal information access;
(7) Children and their guardians also enjoy the rights of personal information subjects as described in Article 5 of this Privacy Policy;
(8) In accordance with domestic laws, regulations, and standards related to personal information, as well as the storage requirements and protection measures outlined in this Privacy Policy, we ensure the security of children's personal information.
(9) To better protect the information of minors, we will designate a dedicated person to be responsible for the protection of children's personal information. If you have any questions, please send an email to the contact information attached in Article 11 to contact us.
8. How can your personal information be transferred globally
In principle, the personal information we collect and generate within the territory of the People's Republic of China will be stored within the territory of the People's Republic of China and transferred overseas only with your authorized consent (if you are a child, we will obtain the authorization consent of the guardian).

We only transmit the minimum amount of personal information necessary to achieve the above purposes, and will strictly comply with the requirements of relevant laws and regulations to protect your personal information, ensuring the security, confidentiality, and integrity of relevant personal information in cross-border transmission.

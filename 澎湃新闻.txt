1、 How do we collect and use personal information
（1） Tips on Personal Sensitive Information
According to the "Personal Information Security Specification for Information Security Technology" (GB/T 35273-2017), personal information refers to various types of information recorded electronically or in other ways that can be used alone or in combination with other information to identify the identity of a specific natural person or reflect the activities of a specific natural person. The personal information covered in this privacy policy includes: basic information (including gender, address, region, personal phone number, email); Personal identification information (including ID card, passport, relevant identification documents, etc.); Network identity identification information (including system account, IP address, password); Personal internet browsing records (including login records, browsing records, and clipboard information); Personal commonly used device information (including hardware model, operating system type, device MAC address, device serial number, software list unique device identification code such as IMEI/Android ID/IDFA/IDFV/OAID/OpenUDID/GUID/GAID/ISMI/Serial/ICCID/MEID/SSID/SN, which describes the basic information of personal commonly used devices); Personal location information (including precise positioning information, latitude and longitude, etc.).
Personal sensitive information refers to personal information that, once leaked, illegally provided, or abused, may endanger personal and property safety, easily lead to damage to personal reputation, physical and mental health, or discriminatory treatment. The personal sensitive information covered in this privacy policy includes: personal identification information (including ID card, passport, relevant identification documents, etc.); Network identity recognition information (including account name, account nickname, user profile picture, and password related to the above); Other information (including personal phone numbers, browsing history, and precise location information). For sensitive personal information, we will prominently mark it in this policy. Please read it carefully.

5、 How to manage your personal information
In order to make it more convenient for you to access and correct your personal information, and to protect your rights to log out, cancel your account, and set privacy functions, in accordance with relevant Chinese laws, regulations, and standards, we have provided you with corresponding operation settings in the product design. You can refer to the following guidelines for operation.
1. Accessing your personal information
When logged in, select the 'My' tab in the bottom right corner of the client, and your profile picture, nickname, and other personal information will be displayed at the top of the page.
2. Correct your personal information
In the logged in state, select the 'My' tab in the bottom right corner of the client, click on it, and your profile picture, nickname, and other personal information area will be displayed at the top of the page to enter your personal homepage. Click the 'Edit Profile' button in the upper right corner again to correct your personal information.
3. Log out and log in
In the logged in state, select the 'My' tab in the bottom right corner of the client and enter 'More Settings'. Pull to the bottom and you will see the 'Log Out' button. Click on it.
4. Cancellation
Method 1: Please send an email to support@thepaper.cn ；
Method 2: In the logged in state, select the "My" tab in the bottom right corner of the client and enter "More Settings". Pull to the bottom and you will see the 'Permanently Cancel Account' button. Click on it and follow the instructions to complete the process.
5. Setting of news content targeted push switch
Select the 'My' tab in the bottom right corner of the client, go to 'More Settings' -' Privacy Settings', and you can turn on or off the 'News Content Targeted Push' switch. After closing, the news you see will not depend on your personalized behavior.
6. Sensitive permission settings
Select the 'My' tab in the bottom right corner of the client, go to 'More Settings' -' Privacy Settings', and you can turn on or off permissions for location, photos (storage space), camera, microphone, obtaining phone information, and making phone calls. After closure, we will no longer collect information related to these permissions. Go to [System Settings] - [Applications] - [Self start Management] to enable or disable the self start function. Disabling it may cause real-time updates of widget content to fail.
